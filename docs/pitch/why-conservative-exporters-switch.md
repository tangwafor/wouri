# Why even a cautious exporter switches to Wouri

**Wouri** · For the operator who has shipped for twenty years on paper, WhatsApp,
and a good memory, and does not trust software. No em-dashes.

You are not wrong to be careful. Most software asks you to change how you work and
gives you a dashboard nobody asked for. Wouri is different, because it is built
around the four things that actually go wrong on a real shipment.

## 1. The container held at the border

A missing **BESC/ECTN** or, for the EU, a missing **EUDR DDS number**, and your
container sits. Wouri keeps a **checklist per shipment** that knows your
destination: it shows exactly what is present and what is missing, and it flags a
loaded shipment with no cargo tracking note before the ship sails. It will not let
you forget the one paper that holds everything.

## 2. The buyer who disputes your certificate

A paper certificate is only as good as the buyer's trust in it. A Wouri document
is **signed and verifiable by anyone, offline, in seconds**, by scanning a code.
Your buyer, their bank, the customs officer, they check it themselves. No calls, no
"send it again." And it is **your** document: your name, your colour, your identity
lead it. Wouri only makes it trustworthy.

## 3. The money that did not come home in time

Under CEMAC rules, proceeds must be repatriated within the window or you face a
penalty. Wouri runs the **BEAC clock** on every consignment and warns you while
there is still time, green, then amber, then red. Settled means repatriated, not
just paid.

## 4. The morning spent chasing what is missing

Instead of five phone calls and a stack of paper, one **board** shows what needs
attention, ranked, most urgent first: a repatriation closing in, a discrepancy, a
missing geolocation, a departure you have not loaded. You see your whole operation
in one glance.

## What you do not have to give up

- **Start where you start.** Buy after harvest from a supplier? That is the first
  option, not an afterthought. You do not need plot data you do not have; Wouri
  flags it to fill later, it does not block you.
- **Nothing to learn.** Describe your business in a sentence, or click. If the
  assistant is ever down, you click. There is no course to pass to ship a lot.
- **Your language.** French first, English if you want, on every screen.
- **It keeps working offline.** Rates and weather show the last known values, the
  field app captures a harvest with no signal, and a certificate verifies with no
  connection at all.

## The one sentence

Wouri does not replace how you export. It makes the file for each shipment
**complete, trustworthy, and impossible to lose**, from the plot to the day the
money comes home. You keep your business; you stop losing containers and time.
