# Wouri: Customer Pitch

For a Cameroonian cocoa and timber exporter, their export manager and documentation officer. French-primary users. Plain, specific, never reassuring without evidence. No em-dashes.

## One-pager

### One file per shipment, from the purchase order to the money coming home.
You run cocoa and timber to Europe. Right now that means one folder for e-GUCE, another for CAMCIS, SIGIF 2 for the logs, the ONCC lab for the cocoa grade, the port, the shippers council, and a bank that bounces your letter of credit two times out of three over a weight that does not match. Wouri puts one shipment in one file, builds every document from the same numbers, and shows you what is stopping the container today. Nobody touches your money.

**Your documents stop contradicting each other.** Enter the weight and the lot once. Wouri writes the invoice, packing list, certificate of origin and customs declaration from that one figure. A packing list that says 18,240 kg can no longer meet an invoice that says 18,420 kg. Letters of credit fail first presentation 60 to 70 percent of the time on mismatches this small. Wouri catches it before your bank sees the file.

**Your EUDR file is built, not improvised.** From 30 December 2026, cocoa and timber entering the EU need a Due Diligence Statement with the GPS polygon of every plot and proof the land was not cleared after 31 December 2020. No reference number, the container is held. Wouri maps the plot once, checks it against the satellite record, assembles the Statement, and keeps the reference number on the shipment.

**For timber, both rails in one place.** Doussie, Acajou and Padouk have needed a CITES export permit since 2023, and the log export ban keeps tightening toward 2028. Wouri runs the EUDR file and the CITES permit and quota side by side on the same shipment.

**A document a buyer in Hamburg can verify without calling you.** Every document carries a code. Your buyer or their bank scans it and sees it is genuine, even with no internet on their side.

**You see the blocker every morning, ranked, with the clock on it.** Each shipment, what is holding it, who owns that step, how many days are left. What the cargo is worth right now at today's rate, your margin, and your 150-day clock to bring the money home.

**You become bankable on your own record.** Pre-export lenders ask for the offtake, the warehouse receipt, the quality reports and documents they can trust. Wouri holds all of it, verified. Wouri never holds or moves your funds.

**It works where you actually work.** Offline, in French, on a cheap phone, syncing when you get back to the road.

**Close.** The EUDR date is fixed and close. The exporters who file a clean Statement, with a reference number, on documents that agree, keep shipping in 2027. Book a 30-minute demo and bring your last rejected shipment; we will show you where Wouri would have caught it.

## Three demo hooks
1. **The weight that never reaches the bank.** Type 18,240 kg on the packing list, start the invoice and fat-finger 18,420. Before you can save, Wouri flags the 180 kg gap and points at both documents.
2. **Scan the document, offline, and watch it verify.** Print a certificate, put the laptop in airplane mode to play the Hamburg buyer, scan the code with a phone. It confirms genuine with no connection.
3. **The morning blocker board with the storm on the window.** Three shipments, ranked, each with its blocker, owner and days left. On the cocoa container the EUDR reference is still missing and the vessel cut-off is in four days, flagged red.
