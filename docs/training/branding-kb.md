# Branding and the knowledge base

**Wouri** · Put your identity on your documents, and (for admins) curate what Aza
knows. No em-dashes.

## Document branding

Who: owner / admin. Time: 2 minutes.

Your certificates should look like yours. On the **Home** page, the **Document
branding** card lets you set:

- A **colour** - used for your monogram and the accent on the certificate.
- A **tagline** - a short line under your name.

Press save. From then on, every document you issue leads with your name, your
colour, and your tagline. Wouri remains the registry that signs and verifies it.

Only an owner or admin can change the brand. It is baked into each document at the
moment it is issued, so a document always shows the brand as it was then.

## The Aza knowledge base

Who: platform admin only. Time: as needed.

Aza knows the whole app: every commodity (with its EUDR and CITES status, quality
parameters, and required documents), the compliance rails, the regulations (EUDR
dates, CITES, the BEAC window, EUR.1), and how each feature works. This knowledge
is **bundled in the app**, so Aza answers even with no connection and no external
service.

A platform admin can keep it current without a code change:

1. Open **/admin/kb** (a link appears on your home when you are a platform admin).
2. Entries are grouped: the app, commodities, compliance rails, regulations,
   capabilities.
3. Edit the explanation (EN and FR), the source, and the review-by date. Press
   **Save**.

Edits are live for tenants at once (the tooltips and onboarding hints read the
edited text), with the bundled copy as the offline floor. Every edit is stamped
with who changed it and when. A non-admin sees the knowledge base read-only; the
database itself refuses their edits, so the read-only view is real.

## How the registry stays current

Regulatory and quality data is **effective-dated**: an update is a new row, and old
documents stay reproducible against the version they were issued under. Every
change is logged. Fast-moving facts (EUDR dates, CITES lists, the BEAC window)
carry a review-by date so nothing goes stale quietly. Exchange rates and weather
are real-time.

## Tips

- Pick a brand colour with enough contrast to read as white initials on it.
- If you are a platform admin, review the fast-moving facts on their review dates.
