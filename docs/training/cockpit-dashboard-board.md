# Cockpit, dashboard, and board

**Wouri** · Seeing everything at a glance. No em-dashes.

Who: owner, export manager. Time: 5 minutes.

## The dashboard (Home)

Your morning view. It answers "what should I care about right now" without
hunting:

- **KPI cards** - consignments, lots, documents, and how many blockers.
- **Needs attention** - the top blockers, ranked, critical first. Click **View
  all** for the full board.
- **Repatriation** - your nearest money clock, or the count overdue, coloured by
  urgency.
- **Recent consignments** and quick actions.

## The readiness board

Open **Readiness board** for the complete, ranked list of what is blocking each
consignment:

- **Repatriation overdue** (critical) - proceeds not home within the BEAC window.
- **Repatriation window closing** (warning) - 15 days or fewer.
- **Settlement discrepancy** (high) - an open mismatch on an instrument.
- **Origin gap** (high) - an EUDR lot with no plot geolocation yet.
- **Overdue task** (high).

Each row shows the consignment, the detail, and the age. Work it top down.

## The cockpit

Open **Cockpit** for the outside world that moves your margins:

- **Exchange rates (XAF)** - live rates against EUR (with the fixed peg shown),
  USD, GBP, and CNY.
- **Weather at key zones** - Douala port and the cocoa, coffee, and timber
  regions, with a **Wet** flag when rain puts drying or transport at risk.

Each panel shows **live** or **cached** with the time. If a source is briefly
unavailable, the cockpit shows the last known values rather than a blank, so you
are never left without a figure.

## Tips

- The dashboard and board read from the same data, so a blocker you clear
  disappears from both.
- Rates and weather refresh on their own; the badge tells you how fresh they are.
