# Getting started

**Wouri** · Sign in, set up your workspace, and find your way around. No em-dashes.

Who: everyone. Time: 5 minutes.

## 1. Create your account

1. Open the app and choose **Create an account**.
2. Enter your email and a password of at least 8 characters. The eye icon shows or
   hides what you type.
3. Confirm your email if asked, then sign in. You can also use **Email me a magic
   link** to sign in without a password.

## 2. Set up your workspace

After signing up you land on the setup page. Two ways, same result:

- **Describe your business.** Type a workspace name, then a sentence like "We
  export cocoa from Bakossi and need the EUDR file." As you type, Aza detects what
  you need and shows the activities it will switch on and the documents you will
  need. Press **Set up my workspace**.
- **Set up by hand.** Type a workspace name and press **Set up by hand**. You pick
  your activities yourself on the next screen.

Aza is a helper, never a gate. If you prefer to click, click. If Aza is ever
unavailable, the by-hand path still works.

## 3. Read the dashboard

Home is your dashboard. At a glance:

- **The numbers** - consignments, lots, documents, and how many things need
  attention.
- **Needs attention** - the ranked list of what is blocking your shipments, most
  urgent first. A red dot is critical.
- **Repatriation** - your nearest money clock, or how many are overdue.
- **Recent consignments** and quick actions to start a lot or a consignment.

If a number looks wrong, that is the point of the dashboard: it is telling you
where to look.

## 4. Find your way around

The bar at the top is on every screen:

- **Home** - the dashboard.
- **Lots** - where your chain starts (harvest or receipt).
- **Consignments** - your shipments, documents, and settlement.
- **Cockpit** - live exchange rates and weather at the export zones.
- **Readiness board** - the full list of blockers.

Top right: switch language between **FR** and **EN** at any time, and **Sign
out**.

## Tips

- The app is bilingual. Pick FR or EN with the toggle; do not rely on your
  browser to translate, it will mangle the labels.
- Hover the small **i** next to any activity to see what it means.
