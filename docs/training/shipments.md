# Shipment tracking

**Wouri** · Following a consignment from the port to arrival. No em-dashes.

Who: export manager, logistics. Time: 5 minutes.

## What it is

A consignment travels. Wouri tracks the journey as milestones, records the
logistics, and warns you when a departure is closing in or an arrival is late.

## Open a shipment

On a consignment detail page, the **Shipment (logistics)** panel:

1. Enter the carrier, the vessel, the ports of loading and discharge, and the
   **ETD** (departure) and **ETA** (arrival).
2. Press **Open shipment**.

## The milestones

A shipment advances in order, and only in order:

**booked → loaded → sailed → arrived → cleared**

Press the next-step button (Load, Sail, Mark arrived, Cleared). The timeline fills
as you go. The consignment status follows: loading marks it **ready**, sailing
marks it **shipped**.

## The board watches your deadlines

Two shipment blockers appear on the readiness board and the dashboard:

- **Departure closing in** (warning) - the ETD is within 7 days and the shipment is
  still only booked. Load and sail it.
- **Arrival overdue** (high) - the ETA has passed and the shipment has not arrived.
  Chase the carrier.

So you never miss a sailing or lose track of a container at sea.

## Tips

- Set the ETD and ETA when you book; the board depends on them.
- The shipment milestone and the settlement clock are separate: one tracks the
  goods, the other tracks the money.
